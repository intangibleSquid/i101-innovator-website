Digital Campaign Process

The first thing that our group created were digital artifacts to represent Radia Perlman. We created posts with outlines from Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. A link to view these artifacts is below.
  Digital Artifacts

The second step for our group was to make a project wireframe. Our wireframes provide information about Radia Perlman and information about us, the creators. A link to our project wireframes is below.
  Project Wireframes

The third step for our group project was to create a survey. The survey asks the typical survey questions, and more questions that are specific to our Digital Artifacts from step 1. You can take a look at our survey at the link below.
  Survey

The fourth step our group did was to send out our survey. We sent the survey to as many people as possible, and recorded their responses on an Excel spreadsheet. We also cleaned the data and created some minor data visualizations.
  Survey Data

Our group did further exercises in data visualization by using Google's Looker Studio. We created some simple data visualizations based on PEW Research Survey Data. To view these Data Visualizations, follow the link below.
  Data Visualization
